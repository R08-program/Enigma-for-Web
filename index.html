Enigma is one of the Cryptographic devices.

Sender decide three rotor gears, alphabet pair to replace, and the alphabets three rotors to start.

Receiver enter the three keys (rotor number, alphabet pair to replace, alphabet which is to start) to enigma decice.

While in this "Enigma for Web", you enter (1) rotor number from 1 to 6, (2) three alphabet pairs to replace, (3) the shift number for each rotor.

The correct answer will be displayed if Receiver enter the correct keys.

Therefore, sender must tell the three keys to receiver beforehand.
